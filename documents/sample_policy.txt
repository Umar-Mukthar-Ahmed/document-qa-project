COMPANY EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK - 2024

SECTION 1: LEAVE POLICY

Annual Leave:
- All full-time employees receive 20 days of paid annual leave per year
- Leave requests must be submitted at least 3 days prior to intended date
- Maximum consecutive leave: 10 days
- Unused leave can carry forward up to 5 days to next year

Sick Leave:
- 10 days of paid sick leave provided annually
- Medical certificate required for absences exceeding 2 consecutive days
- Sick leave cannot be carried forward to the next year

Leave Approval Process:
1. Submit request through HR portal
2. Manager approval required within 48 hours
3. HR sends confirmation email once approved

SECTION 2: WORK FROM HOME POLICY

Eligibility:
- Available to all employees after 3 months of employment
- Up to 2 days per week allowed
- Must have stable internet connection

Requirements:
- Prior approval from direct manager required
- Must be available on communication channels (Slack, Teams) during work hours
- Must attend all scheduled meetings via video call

SECTION 3: WORKING HOURS

Standard Hours:
- Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM
- Lunch break: 1 hour (12:00 PM to 1:00 PM)
- Total: 40 hours per week

Flexible Timing:
- Core hours (must be present): 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM
- Flexible start: Between 8:00 AM to 10:00 AM
- Flexible end: Between 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM

SECTION 4: SALARY AND BENEFITS

Salary Payment:
- Salaries paid on last working day of each month
- Direct bank deposit only
- Salary slips available on employee portal

Benefits:
- Health insurance coverage for employee and immediate family
- Annual performance bonus (up to 15% of annual salary)
- Professional development budget: $1000 per year
- Gym membership reimbursement: Up to $50 per month
